About Krakoa:

    Motto: "Krakoa is for all mutants."

    Official Language: Krakoan

    Governing Body: The Quiet Council

    Description:

    Krakoa is a sentient island living in the middle of the Pacific Ocean. One of two halves of it's parent sentient island, Okarra, Krakoa was settled by the current generation of mutants and succeeded Genosha as a safe haven for all mutantkind. Vitalized by mutant energy, the island-being recognizes mutants as its own and allows those them to travel back and forth to the island using it's "gateways".
