“When you wake from this earthly slumber, my friend, look for me. I will be there, waiting for you, radiant and with open arms.”

About Okkara:

  • Was a large sentient island in the South Pacific that began life as the Enriched (mutant) named Grove from the forgotten Proterozoic Era civilization, Threshold.
  • The Twilight Sword of Amenth split the island-being into two beings, birthing Krakoa and Arakko.
  • Was used as a template by the First Host of Celestials for the creation of The Machine, a subdimensional lattice existing throughout the entirety of the planet Earth built to serve the Eternals.
  • Became a home for the second generation of mutants for over 10000 years.
